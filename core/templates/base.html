About Bende LGA

Bende LGA is the heartbeat of Abia’s heritage—rich in culture, strong in agriculture, and rising as a hub of innovation and community growth. Rooted in tradition yet driven by progress, Bende stands as a proud symbol of unity, resilience, and opportunity in Abia State.
