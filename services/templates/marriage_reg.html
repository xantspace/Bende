Requirements

Originals must be presented on oath-taking day.

  • Birth Certificates (Both)
  • 2 Passport Photos (Both)
  • Valid ID (NIN, Voter's Card)
  • Affidavit of Bachelorhood/Spinsterhood
  • Divorce Certificate (If applicable)

Registry Fee Schedule

  • Celebration Fee: ₦15,000
  • Special License: ₦35,000
